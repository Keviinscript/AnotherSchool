About Us

Get to know us better at Earlystrides Preparatory School

A very warm welcome to The Earlystrides Preparatory School. In our institution you would find brilliant and devoted staff across all our departments, that take our children on a journey of learning and discovery. Our aim is to raise outstanding citizens, who will cause change wherever they go.

We provide a broad and balanced curriculum that allows our students to learn life-long skills and to have aspirations for the future. Within our caring and nurturing environment, we prepare our students to be virtuous, empathetic and innovative. This empowers them to face the complex and ever-changing demands of their immediate society and the world at large.

We pride ourselves as a community that does not only pay attention to its students, but most importantly provide a platform for our staff to grow through Continued Professional Development.

We believe that our holistic approach to education, will make a difference to your ward's path through life.

Once again, welcome!

Mrs. Lola Odion

Director, Earlystrides Preparatory School

Mission Statement

Our mission is to recruit the most outstanding staff, to provide world class learning for all students and to have a 100% pass rate for all external examinations without examination malpractice.

Vision

Our vision is to build an educational institution of international standard using the most modern instructional facilities and driven by highly skilled, motivated and committed workforce.
