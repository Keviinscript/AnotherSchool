Enroll with us at Earlystrides Preparatory School

Our Admission Process

All parents/guardians are required to schedule a visit at the school with the head of administration.

Parents/guardians will each be given an application form, which they are required to fill and return to the school as soon as possible.

To arrange a time for your child or ward to be assessed, contact the head of administration.

The assessment tests for your child or ward will be be held on school grounds. The pass mark for each assessment is a mark of 60%.

If your child or ward does not achieve the passing grade of 60%, you will be asked to meet with the school’s director to discuss the next line of action.
